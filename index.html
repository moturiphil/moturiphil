Passionate Software Engineer specializing in Digital Health Solutions.
Founder & Engineer at Pona.

I focus on developing impactful Health Information Systems (HIS) and SaaS applications. Recently, I led development effort of the Digimal project, which significantly enhanced malaria prevention efforts in Kenya by facilitating the distribution of over 15 million mosquito nets across 22 counties. This involved seamless integration with the Logistics Management Information Systems (LMISs) and the Mpesa platform to optimize distribution and payment processes.

Projects I support include MoH Data Analytics platform to provide actionable insights into healthcare performance, and contributing to the customization of iHRIS, an intergrated human resource system based on FHIR standards, which supports interoperability and efficient health information exchange. I document my journey and share insights on software development, digital health, and more on my blog. Additionally, I am an active contributor to open-source projects and community initiatives in digital health.
